# Turbo Constitution

Start or resume `constitution` with the local runtime, then delegate to `turbo-constitution-facilitator`. Diagnose before asking one question at a time. Preserve the final constitution until the draft is approved; record rejection, contradictions, and pending questions in the interview artifact.
