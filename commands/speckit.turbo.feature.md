description: Drive a feature through product specification, visual analysis when needed, planning, TDD, validation, and review.
---

Treat the request as a feature. Establish the problem, scope, acceptance criteria, assumptions, and non-goals before implementation. Use `$speckit-specify`, `$speckit-clarify`, `$speckit-plan`, `$speckit-tasks`, `$speckit-analyze`, and `$speckit-implement` at their appropriate stages.

When images are attached, run `$speckit-turbo-visual` before the product specification. Pause for configured specification and plan gates. Require `$speckit-turbo-tdd` before implementation when TDD is enabled. Keep traceability from requirement through acceptance, task, code, validation, and review.
