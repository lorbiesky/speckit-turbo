---
description: Facilitate a Socratic creation or update of the project constitution.
---

Diagnose the repository before asking questions: existing constitution, agent guidance, stack, tests, CI, documentation, and conventions. Determine create or update mode. Ask one evidence-based question at a time across engineering, quality, architecture, security, documentation, collaboration, and governance.

Persist the interview in `.specify/memory/constitution-interview.md` and write a proposed `.specify/memory/constitution.draft.md`. Record observed facts, hypotheses, decisions, confidence, contradictions, and open questions. Do not alter `.specify/memory/constitution.md` until every blocking contradiction is resolved and a human explicitly approves the draft. Then invoke the upstream constitution command to apply the approved result.
